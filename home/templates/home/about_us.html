About Startup Gazette

Welcome to The Startup Gazette, an Indian media and information portal renowned for providing comprehensive coverage of the startup ecosystem in India.

Our goal is to strengthen the Indian startup ecosystem by connecting, empowering, and expanding it. We are committed to providing you with the most accurate and recent startup news.

The Startup Gazette was founded in 2022, and since its humble beginnings in Gurgaon and Bangalore, it has come a long way. Its goal is to highlight underutilised entrepreneurial endeavours in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities, coax them out of their shells, and establish connections between them and the top tier of the startup ecosystem.

By covering their entire journey, the startup gazette hopes to fill the gap for startups in tier 2, tier 3, and metropolitan areas.

to provide the newly discovered startup enough vibrancy so that they may be accelerated, incubated, and connected with the appropriate individuals.
